Heart Disease Prediction System

Heart disease (HD) is one of the most common diseases nowadays, due to number of contributing factors, such as high blood pressure, diabetes, cholesterol fluctuation, exhaustion, and many others.

Main aim of this predictive system is to automatically predict whether a human is diagnosed with heart disease or not

Representation of Input

  • - Age (between 20 - 90)
  • - Blood Pressure (between 80-200)
  • - Cholesterol (between 80-300)
  • - Gender (Female = 1 & Male = 0)

Representation of Output

  • - Diagnosed or not
  •     Possible Value 01 –Yes

  •     Possible Value 02 –No
